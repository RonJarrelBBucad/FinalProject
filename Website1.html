Introduction:Climate change refers to the long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. It can be done naturally such as volcanic eruptions, earthquake, etc. It can also be done by humans like burning fossil fuels such as oil, coal and more.

Problem:The problem of this event is that it is not just affecting the Earth but also the people and animals inside it. Climate impacts are already harming health, through air pollution, disease, extreme weather events, forced displacement, pressures on mental health, and increased hunger and poor nutrition in places where people cannot grow or find sufficient food.
